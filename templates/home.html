HOW IT WORKS

Wiconn meaning wireless connect is a free peer-to-peer messaging Application that works with or without internet access or cellular reception to send text and images. Wiconn works by using the Bluetooth and Wifi radios located on your phone to communicate directly with other devices within 300 feet apart. Wiconn works even when mobile telecommunication fails us.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Wiconn App?

Wiconn is a Social Media App that lets you Communicate and Chat with Friends and Family members with or without internet access on your Phone.

What’s the Offline Range of Wiconn?

Wiconn’s offline communication range (i.e. without internet access) is Infinite, as long as they are devices within proximity to connect to. The more people connect to the Network, the faster and more resilient the network becomes. In order to cover a City, approximately 20% of its population should have Wiconn App downloaded on their phones for effective Communication.

How Secure is Wiconn?

At Wiconn we take Privacy and Security very seriously , Wiconn is equipped with top notch End to End Encryption, meaning that only the sender and receiver can view a message except it is a group Chat. Wiconn is also empowered with Self -destruct messaging and ability to prevent people from screen shooting your private messages .

Where Can Wiconn be Used?

Wiconn is effective in times of Natural Disasters Such as Flood, Earth Quakes, Tsunamis whereby in most cases telecommunication infrastructure are destroyed and no way of communication, also Wiconn can be used anywhere from School Campuses, Ships, Estates, Hiking Event and so much more.

How can one Earn Money with Wiconn TV?

You Can Earn money by viewing Adverts which will be displayed on the portion of the App called Wiconn TV, the more time you spend watching those Adverts the more money you earn.

How Long before Wiconn TV pays you?

Users are expected to cash out and wait between 48-72 Hours (This depends on your Bank) to be credited. Please note: You are expected to state your Account name and account number as registered in your Bank.
